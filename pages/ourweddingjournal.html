Plan a Wedding That Resonates

"Our Wedding Journal" walks you through the different steps of wedding planning in a visual, compelling way to help you plan a wedding that resonates with both you and your partner. It encourages creativity and collaboration, with pages for both you and your partner to fill in as you consider the different components that will make your wedding day the most meaningful to you.

Fabian and I started writing and drawing these pages as we planned our own wedding. The various strategies in the journal are based on what worked for us, as well as based on the advice and experiences of other couples we've talked to. It is not a one-size-fits-all guide, but rather a tool to help you plan a wedding that is unique to you and your partner.
