Writing is my passion

A love for creating and exploring new fictitious worlds started when I was little. It all began with my parents reading to my younger brother and me while we ate our dinner.

Coding is my obsession

After the first few lines of code, I was addicted. And I was only programming my TI-84!

And I'm constantly searching for the intersection between the two

When I tell people that I love writing and coding, I get the drastically different responses of "Wow! Those two must be great together!" or "Interesting... Those are very different." I hope that eventually I'll find the perfect balance between writing and coding, and maybe even where they overlap.
